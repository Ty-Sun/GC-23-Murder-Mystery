Knives Out

Beware Carl's murder, where secrets lie entombed.

Once a humble potato, Carl had spent a long day at his office job, dreaming of returning to the comforting embrace of his potato family. Little did he know that fate had a sinister plan in store for him. As Carl trudged home, a serial killer lurking in the shadows pounced upon him. Abducted and cut into pieces, Carl's fragmented remains were hidden away, leaving behind no trace of his existence.


In the shadowed depths of this perplexing enigma, unveil the murder and uncover the elusive remnants of Carl.

Carl was last seen working in the office.
